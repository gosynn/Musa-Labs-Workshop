# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## App: Vertical Stories Platform (Working Title)

## 1. Product Summary
A mobile-first platform for ultra-short, high-engagement vertical video narratives ("microdramas"). Unlike existing platforms focused primarily on romance dramas, this product expands into multiple high-demand genres including action, thriller, comedy, fantasy, sci-fi, documentary, horror, and personal development.

The app integrates AI-driven content creation and A/B testing to produce hit content at scale. Monetization includes tokens, pay-per-episode, subscriptions, and gamified incentives.

---

## 2. Target Users

### Primary Audiences
1. Entertainment binge-watchers who consume short-form vertical videos.
2. Genre enthusiasts (sci-fi, true crime, thriller, documentaries, etc.).
3. Global mobile-first consumers, mainly ages 15–35.
4. Direct-to-consumer superfans willing to pay for premium content.

### Secondary Audiences
5. Studios and creators seeking distribution for AI-produced content.
6. Aspiring filmmakers exploring microstory formats.

---

## 3. Value Proposition
- Expands the vertical-story model beyond romance into multiple categories.
- AI creation and A/B testing enable scalable hit generation.
- Direct-to-consumer monetization and audience ownership.
- Frictionless binge format with 30–120 second episodes.
- Low-cost, high-volume content generation optimized for global audiences.
- Personalized recommendations to drive retention.

---

## 4. Key Features

### 4.1 Home Feed
- Infinite scroll vertical episode feed.
- Personalized algorithm based on viewing behavior and genre preference.
- Labels for trending, new releases, and recommended series.

### 4.2 Content Categories
Supported genres include but are not limited to:
- Romance
- Action
- Thriller
- Fantasy and Sci-Fi
- Horror
- Comedy
- Teen Drama
- Lifestyle
- Documentary Micro-Stories
- True Crime
- Personal Development

Admin can add new genres dynamically.

### 4.3 Series Pages
Each series includes:
- Series overview and synopsis
- Genre tags
- Trailer (optional)
- Episodes list (locked/unlocked)
- A/B version metadata

### 4.4 Episode Viewing
- Full-screen vertical playback
- Auto-play next episode
- Subtitles toggle
- Quick reactions and comments
- CTA prompts for unlocking upcoming episodes

### 4.5 Monetization

#### Token-Based System
- Users purchase coins to unlock episodes.
- Dynamic pricing based on content performance.
- Daily free coin allotment for retention.

#### Subscription Model
- Unlimited access to episodes.
- Monthly and annual plans.
- Early access to new releases.

#### Gamification
- Daily streaks
- XP leveling
- Coin rewards (spin wheel, challenges)
- Mystery boxes

#### Micro-Payments
- Pay-to-unlock premium episodes.
- Discount bundle purchases.
- Genre-specific "passes".

### 4.6 Search and Discovery
- Search by genre, keywords, and vibes.
- Curated playlists ("5-Minute Thrillers", "AI Originals").

### 4.7 Notifications
- Alerts for new episodes
- Series completion reminders
- Personalized recommendations

### 4.8 AI Creator Studio (Phase 2)
- AI script generation
- AI video creation
- Auto-thumbnail generator
- A/B test version manager
- Performance analytics dashboard

### 4.9 A/B Testing Infrastructure
Enables multiple versions of:
- Cuts of episodes
- Thumbnails
- Actor or voice variations
- Pacing adjustments

With automatic rollout of winning variants.

---

## 5. User Flows

### 5.1 First-Time User (FTUE)
1. App download
2. Select genres of interest
3. Watch a free pilot episode
4. Prompt to unlock additional episodes
5. Personalized feed begins

### 5.2 Paying User Flow
1. Encounter paywall
2. Choose to buy coins, subscribe, or earn coins
3. Unlock episode
4. Auto-play encourages longer sessions

### 5.3 Creator Flow (Phase 2)
1. Upload or generate script
2. Generate vertical video
3. Review, edit, and publish
4. Track performance analytics
